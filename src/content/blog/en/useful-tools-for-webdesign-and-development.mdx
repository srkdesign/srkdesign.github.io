Regularly updated collection of useful websites and plugins for work, valuable for both designers and developers.

## Inspiration

1. [Awwwards](https://awwwards.com) — an undisputed classic, the largest curated collection of websites, featuring a regularly updated “Site of the Day.”
2. [Unsection](https://www.unsection.com) — instead of full sites, the collection focuses on individual blocks or sections. Very useful for giving familiar blocks a fresh perspective (e.g., FAQ sections).
3. [App Stacks](https://appstacks.club/web-apps) — one of the few sites in the list that covers not only web but also desktop and mobile app design.
4. [MaxiBestOf](https://maxibestof.one/) — high-quality and unique site collection, filterable by niche and fonts; for inspiration automation, there’s a free weekly newsletter.
5. [Site Of Sites](https://www.siteofsites.co/) — curated collection allowing filtering by niche, site type, style, colors, elements, effects, and animations.
6. [Minimal Gallery](https://minimal.gallery/) — selection of websites in current minimalist style.
7. [CSS Design Awards](https://www.cssdesignawards.com/) — curated collection of functional websites evaluated on UI, UX, and innovation.
8. [One Page Love](https://onepagelove.com/) — large collection of one-page websites and landing pages.
9. [LandBook](https://land-book.com/) — two separate collections (sites and blocks) with extensive filters by colors, fonts, style, industry, and tools.
10. [Godly Website](https://godly.website/) — video collection of incredibly high-quality and creative websites, with subscription options for the best works.
11. [Hover States](https://www.hoverstat.es/) — video collection of interesting hover effects for projects.
12. [Ecomm Design](https://ecomm.design/) — collection of creative e-commerce website designs, filterable by niche and tools.
13. [Footer Design](https://footer.design/) — similar to Unsection, provides a collection of footers from various websites.
14. [Проекты дизайнеров и выпускников UPROCK](https://www.projects.uprock.ru/) — an excellent library of various creative websites in both Latin and Cyrillic scripts.
15. [Dprofile](https://dprofile.ru/) — Russian-language Behance alternative with high-quality works.
16. [Behance](https://behance.com/)
17. [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/)

## Figma Plugins

1. [Photopea](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1164118094004004837) — perfect for quickly editing and creating graphics directly in Figma or any browser. Provides core tools for retouching and editing, similar to Photoshop. May lag on large files or complex elements.
2. [Curve Text](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1331748701210388011) — aligns text along curves or circles with an intuitive interface, easier than other similar plugins.
3. [Charts](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/731451122947612104) — allows creating personalized, easily editable charts in just two clicks.
4. [Perspective Toolkit](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/862059663689780943) — easily create and edit objects in perspective, automatically aligning them to the correct angle.
5. [Beautiful Shadows](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1068595505353552645) — plugin for creating realistic shadows in Figma, allowing soft and natural shadow effects.
6. [Dark Mode Magic](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/834062945643616879) — automatically generates dark mode for your design while maintaining color harmony. Manual adjustments may be needed after generation.
7. [SBOL Typograph](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/907888805660596065) — improves typography by automatically applying rules for spacing, kerning, and alignment.

## Animations

1. [Easings.net](https://easings.net/) — interface for visualizing and testing easing functions.
2. [Cubic-Bezier.com](https://cubic-bezier.com) — interactive tool for creating and adjusting cubic Bézier curves with real-time visualization.
3. [Jitter.video](https://jitter.video/) — similar to Adobe After Effects, intuitive interface for visual animation editing with export options to video, GIF, or Lottie.
